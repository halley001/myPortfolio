About Me

During my academic journey, I have been privileged to receive scholarships that recognized both my potential and my commitment to education. This opportunity enabled me to begin my international education and exposed me to a multicultural learning environment that broadened my academic perspective. The scholarship not only eased the financial burden of studying abroad but also reaffirmed my determination to make the most of every opportunity granted to me.

However, due to financial constraints caused by the crisis in my home country, I was unable to complete my education in Cyprus and had to return to Cameroon to continue my studies at IUGET University. Despite this challenge, I remained focused on my goals and worked hard to prove myself academically. This scholarship served as both financial support and encouragement, reinforcing my belief that resilience and persistence are key to overcoming obstacles.

These two scholarships hold deep personal significance for me. They symbolize not only recognition of past achievements but also a responsibility to strive for excellence in the future. They have motivated me to remain steadfast in the pursuit of my academic and professional aspirations, despite the challenges I have faced. With this support, I continue to push forward, determined to use my education as a tool to contribute meaningfully to society.
